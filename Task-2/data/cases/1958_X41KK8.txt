 PER CURIAM.
This is an appeal from an order of the Federal Communications Commission, granting television Channel 9 in Orlando, Florida, to intervenor Mid-Florida Television Corporation.
Appellant, the unsuccessful applicant, challenges the Commission's action.
After reviewing appellant's contentions, we find no basis for disturbing the Commission's award.
See Tampa Times Co. v. Federal Communications Commission , 1956, 97 U.S.App.D.C.
256 , 230 F.2d 224 .
Affirmed.
